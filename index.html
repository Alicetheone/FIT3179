Fun facts about Olympics

The modern Olympic Games are leading international sporting events and alternating between Summer Olympics and Winter Olympics every 2 years during the 4-year period (Wikipedia, 2022).

Olympic Games Host Countries Count

From the world map of the count of countries have hosted Olympic games during the the year from 1960-2020 above, we could notice that although the Olympics have been held every 2 year, the host countries only varies between several countries, with a majority of developed countries. Africa as a region has even yet to host an Olympic Games.

Brodcast Revenue of Hosting Olympics

As we can see from the chart on the right side, the broadcase revenue a host country can earn out of Summer Olympics Games is generally more than a host country could earn from hosting a Winter Olympic games. We could even notice some of the broadcast revenue of the previous around of Winter Olympics is only half of the boradcast revenue of the summer Olympics Games.

Australia's Medal Win in Olympic Games from 1960-2020

From the area chart of the count of Australia's medal win in Olympic Games, we could observe Australian team usually earn Bronze or Silver more than earning Gold. We could also observe that Australia usually performs better in Summer Olympics Games than Winter Olympics Games with a peak at 2000 Summer Olympic Games hosted in Sydney Australia.

Australia's Gold Medal Win

From the two bar chart + line chart about Australia's Gold Medal Win analysis divided into female and male, we could observe they both have the similar trend as the overall trend above. However, the peak of Male's medal win happened in 2004 Athina Summer Olympics, followed by 1998 Atlanta Summer Olympics. While the peak of Female's medal win happened in 2000 Sydney Summer Olympics. followed by 2008 Beijing Summer Olympics.
