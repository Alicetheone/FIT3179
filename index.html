Facts about Olympics

The modern Olympic Games are a leading international sporting event. They alternate between Summer Olympics and Winter Olympics every 2 years during the 4-year period (Wikipedia, 2022).

Olympic Games Host Countries Count

The world map above shows the count of countries that have hosted the Olympic games during 1960-2020. We notice that although the Olympics have been held every 2 years, the host countries only vary between several countries, with the majority being developed countries. Africa, as a continent, is yet to host a game.

Broadcast Revenue of Hosting Olympics

As we can see from the chart on the right-hand side, the broadcast revenue from a Winter games only generates roughly half the revenue received for the Summer games.

Australia's Medal Win in Olympic Games from 1960-2020

It can clearly be seen above that medal wins increase when the games are held in their home country. Refer the 2000 Summer Olympics held in Sydney.

Australia's Medal Win by Gender

Medal wins by Gender are shown on the left-hand side charts. They both have a similar trend as the overall trend above. However the peak of Male medal wins happened in 2004 Athina Summer Olympics, followed by 1998 Atlanta Summer Olympics. While the peak of Female medal wins happened in 2000 Sydney Summer Olympics, followed by 2008 Beijing Summer Olympics.
